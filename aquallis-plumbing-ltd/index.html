About Aquallis Plumbing

Aquallis Plumbing Ltd is a second-generation family plumbing business founded in 2019 by Ben, an experienced plumbing engineer with years of hands-on expertise in boilers, heating systems & emergency repairs.

We serve Coventry & the wider West Midlands, specialising in boiler repairs, installations, central heating & emergency plumbing. As an official Viessmann installer, we're proud to fit premium German boilers with extended warranties & expert technical support.

Our commitment is simple: fast response times, honest advice & professional workmanship. Whether you need a routine service, an emergency callout or a complete heating system overhaul, Aquallis is here 24/7 to keep your home warm & your water flowing.
