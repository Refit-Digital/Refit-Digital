What We Do

Auto Electrical Services in Portsmouth

From a single blown fuse to a full rewire — our expert team diagnoses and resolves auto electrical faults on all makes and models.

Auto Electrical Diagnostics

Advanced fault-finding on warning lights, ECU faults, sensor failures, and intermittent electrical issues that other garages struggle to pin down.

Vehicle Rewiring

Full or partial vehicle rewiring for cars, vans, and commercial vehicles. We repair damaged looms, trace short circuits, and restore full electrical function.

Central Locking & Alarms

Supply, fitting, and repair of central locking systems and vehicle security alarms. We also diagnose and fix faults with existing remote-locking setups.

Battery & Charging Systems

Battery testing, supply and fitting, and full alternator and charging circuit diagnosis. Including stop-start battery replacements and recalibrations.

Starter & Alternator Repair

Diagnosis and repair or replacement of starter motors and alternators. We test your charging system thoroughly to get to the root cause of the fault.

Dashboard & Instrument Repairs

Faulty instrument clusters, warning lights, speedometers, and dashboard electrics diagnosed and repaired. Including storage heater controls and interior electrics.

Lighting & LED Upgrades

Headlight and tail-light repairs, DRL faults, LED conversions, trailer lighting, and all exterior and interior vehicle lighting issues sorted quickly.

Air Conditioning Electrics

Diagnosis of electrical faults within air conditioning systems — from faulty fans and blower motors to control unit issues and climate control electrics.
