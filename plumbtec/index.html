About PlumbTec

Your Local Plymouth Plumber

PlumbTec is a family-run plumbing and gas engineering service based in Plymouth, built on a simple promise: when you need a plumber, we'll be there — fast, professional, and fair.

Our team, including Rich, Shane, and Hawker, covers everything from emergency burst pipes to full bathroom installations and boiler replacements. We're Gas Safe registered, fully insured, and we take pride in leaving every job spotless.

What sets us apart is genuine 24/7 availability. We know that a boiler breakdown at 2 AM or a blocked drain on Christmas morning needs fixing now, not next Tuesday. That's why we've structured our team to always have someone ready to take your call.
