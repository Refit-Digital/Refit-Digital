What We Do

Our Auto Electrical Services

From a simple battery swap to complex fault-finding across the entire vehicle loom — we've seen it all and fixed it all.

Diagnostics & Fault Finding

Pinpointing electrical faults fast with professional diagnostic equipment. No guesswork, no unnecessary parts.

Vehicle Rewiring

Full and partial loom repairs, custom wiring and harness fabrication. Restoring electrical integrity to your vehicle.

Battery & Charging

Battery testing, supply and fitting. Alternator and charging system diagnostics to keep you running reliably.

Starter & Alternator

Diagnosis and replacement of starters and alternators. We source quality parts at fair prices to keep costs down.

Lighting & LED Upgrades

Headlights, tail lights, indicators and interior LED upgrades. MOT-compliant repairs and modern conversions.

Android Auto & Entertainment

Head unit installations, Android Auto, Apple CarPlay, speakers and amplifiers. Modernise your in-car experience.

Towbar Electrics

Towbar wiring for trailers and caravans. Single and twin electrics, coded to your vehicle's ECU where needed.

MOT Electrical Repairs

Failed your MOT on an electrical fault? We fix lights, sensors, dashboard warnings and wiring to get you back on the road.
