What We Do

Our Plumbing & Heating Services

From a dripping tap to a full central heating installation — we handle it all with care and professionalism.

Boiler Installation

Expert fitting of new, energy-efficient boilers. We advise on the best model for your home's needs and handle the full installation to Gas Safe standards.

Boiler Repair & Servicing

Fast, reliable diagnosis and repair of boiler faults. Annual servicing keeps your system running efficiently and your warranty intact.

Central Heating Systems

Full installation, upgrade, or repair of central heating systems. We ensure every radiator heats evenly and your system operates at peak efficiency.

Gas Safety Certificates

Landlord CP12 certificates and full gas safety inspections carried out by our Gas Safe registered engineers. Keep your tenants safe and your property compliant.

Emergency Plumbing

Burst pipes, leaks, loss of water — we respond quickly and work flexible hours to sort urgent plumbing problems, including evenings when needed.

Bathroom & Kitchen Plumbing

Tap installation and replacement, sink and basin fitting, shower connections, waste pipe work, and all associated plumbing for bathroom and kitchen projects.

Radiator Installation & Balancing

Supply and fit of new radiators, TRV upgrades, and full system balancing to eliminate cold spots and reduce your heating bills.

Power Flushing

Restore full heating efficiency by removing sludge and debris from your system. Particularly effective before a new boiler installation or if radiators are taking too long to heat up.

Newcastle Plumbing & Heating Ltd was established in March 2008 by Tom, a Gas Safe registered engineer with a passion for doing things properly.
