What We Do

Plumbing Services in Wolverhampton

From a dripping tap to a full bathroom fit-out — we handle all domestic plumbing work across the West Midlands.

Emergency Plumbing

Burst pipes, major leaks, or loss of water — we respond quickly and work outside normal hours when needed to resolve urgent plumbing problems fast.

Boiler Repair & Servicing

Fault diagnosis, repair, and annual servicing for all makes and models. Keeping your boiler in top condition means lower bills and fewer breakdowns.

Bathroom Plumbing & Fitting

Full bathroom plumbing installations — baths, showers, basins, WCs, and waste connections. We also work alongside tilers and decorators for full refurbs.

Leak Detection & Repair

Visible or hidden leaks tracked down and fixed properly — including under-sink leaks, pipe joints, and external overflows. Quick, tidy, and long-lasting repairs.

Central Heating Systems

Installation, upgrades, and repairs to central heating systems. We balance radiators, fit TRVs, and ensure your home heats evenly and efficiently.

Blocked Drains

Slow or completely blocked drains cleared quickly and cleanly. Sinks, baths, showers, and outdoor drains — we get the water flowing again.

Tap & Pipework

Tap replacement, new tap installation, pipework runs, and isolation valve fitting. Whether it's a single dripping tap or new pipework for a kitchen, we cover it.

Gas Safety Certificates

Landlord CP12 gas safety inspections carried out by qualified engineers. Keep your rental property compliant and your tenants protected.
